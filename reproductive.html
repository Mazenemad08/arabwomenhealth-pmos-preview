Reproductive Health Associated with PCOS/PMOS

Is it possible to get pregnant if I have PCOS/PMOS?

While PCOS/PMOS can impact reproductive health due to hormonal imbalances, there are medical treatments, fertility medications, and egg donation options available to assist those with PCOS/PMOS in conceiving. If you're struggling to start a family due to PCOS/PMOS, it's important to work closely with your doctor to find the right path forward. Celine® may also help manage the hormonal imbalances and related reproductive issues associated with PCOS/PMOS.

Should I be worried about PCOS/PMOS only if I plan to have a baby in the future?

PCOS/PMOS can have effects beyond reproductive health, potentially impacting a woman throughout her life. It is associated with type 2 diabetes, gestational diabetes, high blood pressure, unhealthy cholesterol levels, and even endometrial cancer.

Therefore, getting diagnosed and managing PCOS/PMOS is very important for overall health. While certain lifestyle changes can be beneficial, using a specially formulated supplement to manage the hormonal imbalance of PCOS/PMOS may enhance your reproductive health and improve your chances of conceiving.
