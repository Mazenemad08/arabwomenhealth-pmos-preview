Fatima’s Journey with PCOS/PMOS – Through the Eyes of Her Doctor
Let me share an inspiring story from my clinic – Fatima’s journey. Fatima, a woman in her late thirties, came to my clinic carrying the dream of motherhood.

Years of failed attempts and fertility challenges had taken a toll on her, compounded by issues like excessive hair growth and hair loss, which affected her confidence.

When Fatima walked into the clinic, her exhaustion was evident. She shared her story and the pressures she had endured. After running tests, we discovered that she was suffering from Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS/PMOS).

The diagnosis was a relief for her, as it explained her struggles, but it also posed a challenge requiring serious steps to improve her condition.We created a treatment plan, and incorporating the supplement Celine was pivotal. It helped address key PCOS/PMOS factors such as hormonal imbalances, insulin resistance, and increased oxidative stress.

I also encouraged her to adopt a healthy lifestyle, including a balanced diet and regular exercise.

With the support of her husband and their shared patience, Fatima’s condition gradually improved, and over time, her dream came true.

After years of challenges, Fatima welcomed her first child amidst overwhelming joy from her family.

Fatima’s journey with Celine in overcoming PCOS/PMOS and achieving her dream of motherhood became a symbol of transformation from imbalance to balance.
