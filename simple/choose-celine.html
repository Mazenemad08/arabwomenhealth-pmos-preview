In what ways can Celine support the management of my PCOS/PMOS symptoms?

Celine® is an easy-to-use 5-in-1 supplement designed to address imbalances related to PCOS/PMOS, including high androgen levels (male sex hormones), insulin resistance and reproductive health issues.

1.Alpha-lipoic acid

This is a powerful antioxidant which reduces oxidative stress and helps to promote healthy metabolism of sugars. It may also help to reduce your body weight, body mass index and waist circumference. This can also help you to manage potential insulin resistance and other associated health risks.

2.Myo-Inositol

This helps to reduce insulin resistance in women with PCOS/PMOS, and therefore may help with hormonal imbalance. Myo-Inositol improves oocyte quality which can improve period irregularities as well as chances of conception. Myo-inositol and alpha-lipoic acid work together to help improve reproductive health.

3.AFolic Acid

This helps the body to metabolize proteins, aids in forming red blood cells and also helps to prevent neural tube defects (birth defects) in unborn babies.

4.N-acetyl cysteine

This is a crucial component of PCOS/PMOS supplements because it plays a dual role in reducing insulin sensitivity and oxidative stress, both of which are significant factors in the development of PCOS/PMOS. Additionally, numerous studies support its effectiveness in enhancing ovulation and increasing pregnancy rates.
